HOW TO ATTEND THE SHOW

Option 1: Livestream on Twitch

Watching the show is as simple as navigating to the Twitch channel for Loner ONLINE!

When the show is starting the stream will automatically pop up. When the show is done, the replay video will be available under Recent Videos on Twitch.

Option 2: Join in VRChat

If you have a PC and have VRChat installed (Windows only), you can join in the fun right in game! The exact details of how to join in game are provided on the day of the event on the Loner Discord. To get started, join the Discord server and instructions will be provided on how to join.

What is Loner?

Collaborative club event of Zeal and Laces, looking to bring alternative styles of electronic music to Melbourne's club scene.
